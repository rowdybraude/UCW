Welcome!

Welcome to the landing page for the final project of Group 1: The Serpents™.

The goal of this project was to demonstrate the relationship between the Family Federation for World Piece and Unification (FFWPU or Unification Church) and the various approaches, definitions, and themes regarding religion outlined in this course.

Broadly, this project aims to apply essentialist, functionalist, and family resemblance definitions to highlight that yes, UNIFICATION IS A RELIGION.

Beyond that, however, we also aim to demonstrate how Unification manifests religiously, operating similtaneously as an organized, centralized religion with deeply embodied practices, and as a massive multimedia conglomerate which straddles the definition of relgiousity.
