Unification in the WRP

Criteria

Scripture | Beliefs/Morals | Inner Experiences | Structure/Organization

The religion Is centered on the teachings and scripture of Sun Myung Moon.

  • Primarily his book Exposition of The Divine Principle but also Moon's speeches are considered authoritative to the belief.
  • The christian bible is loosely referenced but is not the center of scripture.
  • The divine principle is a study of creation which aims at understanding the creator.

The church has specific practices and ceremonies.

  • Blessing Ceremony (mass weddings):
    • Consists of 5 steps: chastening ceremony, wine ceremony, vows and ring exchange, 40 abstinence after marriage (separation period), blessing ceremony.

The church has structure and organization.

  • Moon and his wife was/is leader of the church.
  • Church has many companies it owns as well as affiliate organizations that promote its values.
  • Existence of official Church body and full-time members who work for the Church.

The church adheres to central morals/beliefs which followers must obey and which are central to the community; things taken as truth

  • Its 3 core ideals: Family, World peace, unification.
  • Belief in God and a creator:
    • Everything in nature comes in pairs (male female, + and -) - based on Moon's observations.
    • God's character is characterized by equal masculine and feminine aspects joined in perfect harmony.
    • Since humans value love and harmony “heart” is the essence of God's character.
  • The purpose of creation and life is to enjoy love - we are meant to live out and give perfect love to others and be in perfect love in relation to God.
  • We have fallen as a race because instead of love we seek selfishness and corruption- due to Adam and Eve's fall.
  • Overall family values are promoted to be the most important aspect of society and the religion.

The official symbol of the Unification Church highlights a clear effort to organize Church dogma: The 12 inner lines represent the 12 gates to heaven, while the 4 larger arrows represent the 4 cardinal directions. The outer arrows represent yin and yang respectively. Shows the Unification Church not only has an organized group, but as a syncretized group as well.
